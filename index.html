Incredible India

Ancient India

Ancient India was home to the Indus Valley Civilization, one of the world's oldest civilizations. India gave the world important inventions like zero, yoga, and Ayurveda.

Medieval India

Medieval India saw the rise of powerful kingdoms and the Mughal Empire. Famous rulers like Akbar and Shah Jahan contributed to art, culture, and architecture.

Modern India

India fought for independence against British rule. Great leaders like Mahatma Gandhi, Bhagat Singh, and Subhash Chandra Bose played important roles in freedom. India became independent on 15 August 1947.
