About The Fort

Kumbhalgarh Fort is one of the most famous forts in Rajasthan. It was built by Rana Kumbha in the 15th century and is surrounded by huge walls spread over many kilometers.

Explore More

Special Features

The wall of Kumbhalgarh Fort is known as the second longest wall in the world after the Great Wall of China.

Maharana Pratap

Maharana Pratap was born in Kumbhalgarh Fort. The fort is an important symbol of Rajput bravery and history.
