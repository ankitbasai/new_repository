About Maharana Pratap

Maharana Pratap was one of the greatest warriors in Indian history. He fought bravely against the Mughal Empire to protect Mewar and Rajput pride.

Battle of Haldighati

The Battle of Haldighati was fought in 1576 between Maharana Pratap and Akbar's army led by Man Singh. Maharana Pratap showed unmatched bravery during the battle.

Chetak - The Loyal Horse

Chetak was the loyal horse of Maharana Pratap. Even after being injured, Chetak saved Maharana Pratap's life and became a symbol of loyalty and courage.
